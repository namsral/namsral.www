Will Google TV Become An Open Source Project »
posted on May 1, 2011 #

Google TV sales according to Logitech’s CEO:

CEO Gerald P. Quindlen told investors in prepared remarks Thursday that he’s still “enthusiastic about Google TV.” He also said that Logitech has scaled back marketing for its Google TV product, but added the company was “fully prepared to re-accelerate those activities at the appropriate time.”

In other words, Google TV is a dead end at Logitech.

Regarding the 2nd generation of Google TV:

Part of this next generation will be apps and access to the Android Market

If the apps and Android Market will be as successful as they have been on Android based tablets, Google TV will go nowhere and soon become yet another one of Google’s open source projects.
