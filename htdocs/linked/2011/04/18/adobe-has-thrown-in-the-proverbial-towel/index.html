Adobe Has Thrown In The Proverbial Towel »
posted on April 18, 2011 #

We also began previewing the next version of Adobe Flash Media Live Encoder, which can capture a live broadcast stream and publish out to multiple devices including Android, Apple iOS and Samsung TVs.

It seems Adobe has thrown in the proverbial towel when it comes to Flash video. One of its media server solutions was upgraded to support the open video standard H.264 used by Apple’s iOS devices to play online videos.

Meanwhile, Google still awaits massive WebM adoption and a decent Flash implementation for Android.
