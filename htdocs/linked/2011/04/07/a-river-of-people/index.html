A River of People »
posted on April 7, 2011 #

A fascinating documentary which starts off with the largest human migration. Roughly 130 million migrant workers in China travel from the cities to the countryside to spend the New Year holidays with the families they left behind. Imagine a third of the US population traveling from the coastal areas to the rural areas before Christmas.
