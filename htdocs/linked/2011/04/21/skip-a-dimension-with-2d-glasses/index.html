Skip A Dimension With 2D Glasses »
posted on April 21, 2011 #

How they work is a bit complicated, but the end effect is that you get to watch a 3D movie as if it’s a normal 2D movie, eliminating eyeball strain.

Great for people who get 3D sick or 3D haters like myself. Every theater should have these glasses as an option out for 3D.
